An art-centered personal website that I am willing to share with everyone especially the young aspiring artists like me, to help and motivate them with their art journey and hopefully serve as their inspiration. It is for people who wants to learn how to draw, improve their art skills, and expand their creativity.

I am Marinella O. Moral. I'm an 18 year old college student with the course of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I made this platform to share my knowledge to every people who have a passion for arts. And let me remind you that whatever career you are in right now, you can still continue your passion.
Artgenda is short for Art Agenda, according to Cambridge Dictionary, Agenda is defined as a list of aims or possible future achievements. The word agenda is the plural for of the Latin word agendum, which literally means "something to be done." On the other hand, Art has a broad meaning, Art is more than a hobby, it is a burning passion that lights a fire in people's souls. For me, your passion has a lot of something to be done, you should hone it and be proud of it, and the most important thing is that, never let the fire in your passion fades away.
